Quick Motor Vehicle Search

For an easier search, you can use the PPSR Car Check, which gives you important details in a simpler format and is optimised for mobile use.
Try PPSR Car Check here.

A motor vehicle search using its VIN or Chassis number will show you if a vehicle has any interests registered. It may also tell you if the vehicle is recorded as stolen or written off. This information comes from a system called the National Exchange of Vehicle and Driver Information System (NEVDIS).

Important: NEVDIS data is not always be available or up-to-date when you search on the PPSR.

Use of the PPSR is subject to the PPS Register General Conditions of Use, Third Party Data Conditions (for example NEVDIS data conditions) and the appropriate fees.
