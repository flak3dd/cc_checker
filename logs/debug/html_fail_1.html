Important Note

Please enter your driver's licence number or your vehicle plate number to retrieve the account number. The account number will be required in order to make payment for a current invoice(s) for your driver's licence or vehicle licence. Please note that the account number changes with each invoice and can only be used once.

Please be aware that your driver's licence will only retrieve driver's licence related invoices and likewise your vehicle licence will only retrieve vehicle related invoices, for example; vehicle transfer, vehicle licence renewal, plate infringement and final demand notices, vehicle transfer infringement and vehicle transfer final demand notices.

Results displayed for vehicle licences will be limited to accounts applicable to the current owner of the vehicle. Those that apply to the previous owner/s will be excluded.

Once a valid account number and relevant details are obtained, payment can be made for that account either at the Online Payments Page or via the telephone on 1300 655 322.
